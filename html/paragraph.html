About Us

An IT training company in Calicut, BAABTRA, works as a link in identifying, training and placing quality freshers from regional colleges and institutes. A firm that specialises in identifying, training and placing skilled resources from districts such as Kozhikode, Kannur, Malappuram, Wayanad and Kasargode for IT jobs in Calicut, Cochin, Trivandrum, Bangalore and middle eastern cities, BAABTRA provides training in technologies such as Android, PHP and Python in a totally industry oriented manner. We teach you to join companies; we let companies interact with the talent pool in domain-sensitive ways from six offices in Calicut connected to our Bangalore office as well.

Vision

BAABTRA envisages a world in which talent, innovation and industry connect seamlessly for better technologies, resources and lives.

Mission

In our mission to find, prepare and produce excellent hands with a strong foundation in the domain of programming for the IT industry, we seek to heighten learning from a computing perspective.

Services

We are into IT training and resource development and provide our service to various engineering colleges, education institutes and corporate MNC’s for the past four years. We research skills gaps and from there we then design and develop all of our training courses in great detail with one aim in mind ­ to help you take your course participants and your company to the next level.
